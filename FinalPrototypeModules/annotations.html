• Annotations

Via annotations, journalists from different disciplines can cooperate while writing an article. By using annotations similar to Google Docs, journalists can improve their article.


How we can implement it in our tool:
Within the editor mode, journalists will have the option to insert annotations to their own work, but with permission, also to the work of others. This can also be useful for the editorial office, to annotate the work that has been sent to them and show where some adaption might be needed.

What's the benefit for the journalist?
By using annotations, the journalist can improve their own work, but can also learn from others by co-operating.
