• Overview

On this webpage, all the different modules from the project ‘Digital Publishing Tools for Journalists’ are gathered. The journalist of the future can use these modules to organize their information in a better way and work more efficiently.

At the same time, the modules help to expand the lifespan of the stories that the journalist of the future creates. For now, we stored them all together on this website. When our prototype is programmed or made clickable, we are going to implement the individual modules into our prototype.
